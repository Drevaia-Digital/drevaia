Why do I feel sad for no reason?

Sometimes sadness appears without an obvious cause. Nothing specific happened today, yet something inside feels heavy.

Emotions are not always immediate reactions. Many feelings come from deeper layers of our experiences, memories, and unmet needs.

Feeling sadness does not mean something is wrong with you. Often it is a quiet signal from within, asking for attention and care.

Listening to that feeling can be the first step toward understanding yourself with more compassion.
