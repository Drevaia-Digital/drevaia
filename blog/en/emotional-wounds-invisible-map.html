The Invisible Map

What is not healed does not disappear.
It adapts. It reorganizes. And then it directs.

Emotional wounds do not vanish with time. They simply become invisible rules we live by.

The past is not behind you. It lives in the way you react, the way you love, the way you protect yourself.

Healing is not forgetting.
Healing is seeing clearly what was shaping you in silence.

Many people believe they are choosing freely… when in reality, they are repeating what hurt them.

The wound becomes a map. And until you recognize it, it keeps drawing your reality.
