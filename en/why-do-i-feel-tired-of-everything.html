Why do I feel tired of everything?

Sometimes the exhaustion is not only physical. It is emotional.

You may feel like everything requires too much energy: decisions, responsibilities, or even simple daily tasks.

This kind of fatigue often appears when we have been carrying too much for too long without space to rest.

Recognizing that exhaustion is not weakness. It is a sign that something inside needs care and attention.
