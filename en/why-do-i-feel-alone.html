Why do I feel alone?

Feeling alone does not always mean that there are no people around you. Sometimes it appears even when you are surrounded by others.

Emotional loneliness often appears when a part of us does not feel seen, heard or understood.

It is not a personal failure. It is a signal asking for attention.

Understanding that feeling can be the first step toward reconnecting with yourself.

When loneliness appears

Sometimes loneliness has nothing to do with the number of people around us, but with the feeling of not being truly understood.

It may appear when we learn to silence what we feel, when we carry too much in silence, or when we slowly move away from ourselves.

Listening to that feeling is not weakness. Often it is the beginning of returning to yourself.
