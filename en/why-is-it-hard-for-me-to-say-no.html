Why is it hard for me to say no?

Many people struggle with saying no. They say yes even when they feel tired, overwhelmed, or uncomfortable.

Often this happens because we learned that being accepted meant pleasing others.

But boundaries are not rejection. They are a form of self-respect.

Learning to say no does not make you selfish. It helps you protect your time, your energy, and your emotional well-being.
