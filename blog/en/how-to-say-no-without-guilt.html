How to Say No

Without guilt.
Without disappearing.

Many people do not struggle with saying yes.
They struggle with the fear of what happens if they say no.

Because for a long time, love felt conditional.
Approval felt like survival.

Saying no is not rejection.
It is self-respect.

Boundaries are not cruelty.
They are the moment you stop abandoning yourself to be accepted.

You are not selfish for protecting your inner space.
You are finally present.
