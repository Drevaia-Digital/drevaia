Boundaries

Are not rejection.
They are remembrance.

Some people think boundaries are walls.
But most of the time, boundaries are doors.

Not to keep others out.
But to keep yourself in.

A boundary is the moment you stop disappearing
to make love stay.

You are not cold for needing space.
You are not cruel for protecting your peace.

You are learning that love should not require self-erasure.
