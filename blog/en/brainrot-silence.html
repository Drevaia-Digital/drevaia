Brainrot

When noise never stops, the soul forgets how to speak.

Brainrot is not about screens.
It is about what happens when silence disappears.

When the mind never empties, it forgets how to listen.
When attention is always occupied, the soul becomes background noise.

Children are not losing intelligence.
They are losing stillness.
Adults are not distracted.
They are exhausted.

Silence is not absence.
It is the space where meaning is formed.

When silence is stolen, identity fragments.
What remains is reaction without reflection.
