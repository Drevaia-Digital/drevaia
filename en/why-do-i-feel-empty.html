Why do I feel empty?

Sometimes the feeling is not sadness. It is silence. As if something inside us stopped responding.

Many people describe this experience as an inner emptiness. It does not always appear for an obvious reason.

Emotional emptiness can appear when we spend too much time disconnected from what we truly feel, or when something in our life no longer has the same meaning.

When emotional emptiness appears

Feeling empty does not always mean that something is missing in your external life.

Sometimes it means that a part of you needs to be heard, understood, or reconnected with what truly matters.

Emptiness can also appear during moments of change, emotional exhaustion or a search for meaning.

Reflections that may accompany you

  • Whispers — for days when everything feels noisy inside
  • Embraces — for when being strong becomes exhausting

Questions often connected with this feeling

  • Why do I feel lost?
  • Why do I feel sad for no reason?
  • Why do I feel lonely?

Sometimes emptiness is not something to eliminate, but something to listen to.
